Consulting Services

I provide technical support for research groups, agribusinesses and institutions seeking to implement data-driven water management and precision agriculture.

ET & CWSI Modeling

Design and implementation of workflows for evapotranspiration (ETa/ETc) and Crop Water Stress Index (CWSI) using METRIC, TSEB, AquaCrop and thermal imagery (UAV + satellites).

Remote Sensing Pipelines

Development of Google Earth Engine scripts and Python workflows for multitemporal analysis with Sentinel-2 and Landsat 8/9: vegetation indices, texture metrics, land use and crop monitoring.

UAV Campaigns & Calibration

Planning and execution of UAV thermal and multispectral campaigns, radiometric calibration (ThermiCAL), RTK workflows and integration with field data and weather stations.

Training & Workshops

Tailored courses on precision agriculture, remote sensing, GEE, Python and UAV-based monitoring for universities, research centers and companies.

Research Works

My consulting and advisory work is supported by peer-reviewed research in Q1 international journals.

Rice Yield Prediction with UAV Spectral & Textural Indices

Machine learning models using multispectral and textural features derived from UAV imagery to predict rice yield in Lambayeque, Peru.

Water Use Efficiency under AWD with UAV & AquaCrop

Evaluation of water use efficiency in rice using alternative wetting and drying (AWD), energy balance models, UAV thermal data and AquaCrop.

CWSI & Stomatal Conductance with Thermal Sensors

Monitoring water stress under different irrigation regimes using thermal imagery and stomatal conductance measurements on the northern coast of Peru.
